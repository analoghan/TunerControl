Stoichiometric Reference Values

Gasoline: AFR 14.7:1 = Lambda 1.000

E85: AFR 9.765:1 = Lambda 1.000

Lambda is fuel-independent — Lambda 1.0 always means stoichiometric regardless of fuel type. AFR values differ because each fuel requires a different mass of air per unit of fuel.

Common Tuning Targets

WOT / Full Power (Gasoline): Lambda 0.82–0.88 (AFR 12.0–13.0)

WOT / Full Power (E85): Lambda 0.82–0.88 (AFR 8.0–8.6)

Cruise / Part Throttle: Lambda 1.00–1.05 (stoich or slightly lean)

Lean Best Torque: Lambda 0.86–0.90 (AFR 12.6–13.2 gasoline)

Cat Protection Limit: Lambda >1.10 — avoid sustained lean operation above this
